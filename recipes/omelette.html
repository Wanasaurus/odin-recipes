Description

I like to make omelettes. They are easily customisable, not difficult, taste great, and are healthy (depending on what you put inside!). This omelette dish is something that regularly appears on my family dinner table. If your frying pan is too wide to make a nice round omelette, you can scramble the eggs instead. The dish will still taste good!

Ingredients

This recipe serves four.

  • Four eggs
  • Half a packet of snake beans (use string beans if you can't find snake beans)
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Two tablespoons of fish sauce (you can also use soy sauce)
  • One tablespoon of mirin (omit if you have dietary restrictions)
  • One tablespoon of sake/cooking ric wine (omit if you have dietary restrictions)
  • Two tablespoons of neutral flavoured cooking oil e.g. canola oil
  • Pepper to taste
